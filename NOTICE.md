## Human authorship

This project was designed and directed by Yuxuan Lin 林榆璇. The human creative
contributions include:

- The overall concept and product design of the tool.
- The Eisenhower quadrant layout, the urgency/importance axes, and the
  red→green priority color scheme (Q1 Crisis, Q2 Goals & Planning,
  Q3 Interruptions, Q4 Distractions).
- The system architecture: the Tasks sheet as the single source of truth, the
  read-only Board View, and the design of the sheet-to-calendar sync model.
- Key technical direction, including identifying and specifying the fix for the
  event-ID collision behavior in the sync logic.
- The selection, arrangement, and coordination of all components (spreadsheet,
  script, documentation, and landing page) into a working whole.
- The choice of license and attribution terms, and the visual/aesthetic direction
  of the landing page.
